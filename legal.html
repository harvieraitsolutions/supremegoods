Privacy & Data Sovereignty

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Extended Data Collection Transparency

We collect only information strictly necessary for order processing, fraud prevention, shipping logistics, taxation compliance, and payment gateway authorization. Data is processed under lawful basis including contract performance, legal obligation, and legitimate interest.

  • Full legal name and verified contact information.
  • Billing and shipping address validation records.
  • Payment authorization tokens from TakePayments (never raw card numbers).
  • Device fingerprinting for fraud detection.
  • Order history and transactional logs.
  • IP address, browser version, operating system metadata.
  • Customer service communications.
  • Refund and dispute records.
  • AML screening data (if required by payment provider).

Data Retention Policy

Transaction records are securely retained for 6 years to comply with UK financial and tax legislation. Fraud detection logs may be retained longer where legally required.

International Transfers

If payment processing requires cross-border transfer, appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are implemented.

Terms of Service

Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining confidentiality of account credentials and for all activity conducted under their account.

Payment Authorization

By submitting payment details, you authorize Supreme Goods Ltd and TakePayments to perform verification checks including 3D Secure authentication, AVS validation, CVV verification, and anti-fraud scoring.

Order Verification

  • Manual review may be conducted for high-value orders.
  • Additional ID verification may be requested.
  • Orders flagged as high risk may be canceled.
  • Chargeback abuse may result in account suspension.

Prohibited Activities

  • Card testing attempts.
  • Use of stolen payment credentials.
  • False chargeback claims.
  • System probing or vulnerability scanning.
  • Reselling products without authorization.

Dispute Resolution

All disputes must first be addressed through our internal resolution team before initiating chargebacks.

Limitation of Liability Expansion

Maximum liability shall not exceed total amount paid for the relevant order.

Shipping & Refund Framework

Shipping Compliance

  • All shipments insured during transit.
  • Tracking numbers provided upon dispatch.
  • Signature required for high-value deliveries.
  • Delivery timeframe estimates are non-binding.
  • International customs duties are buyer responsibility.

Refund Eligibility

  • Item must be unused.
  • Original packaging required.
  • Serial numbers must match invoice.
  • No physical damage caused by misuse.
  • RMA authorization required prior to return.

Chargeback Policy

Customers agree not to initiate fraudulent chargebacks. Evidence including IP logs, signature confirmation, and payment authentication data will be submitted to banks in dispute cases.

Refund Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are issued within 5-7 business days via original payment channel. Processing time may vary depending on card issuer.
